Studio

B. 1990 in Vancouver BC, Tom Chung is an independent industrial designer who established his eponymous studio in 2016. Based in Toronto the studio maintains a focus on material objects and interior space with an emphasis on experience. Forming a unique design process which combines contemporary culture and local industry, the studio creates context driven industrial design pieces for domestic, institutional and public environments.
